Our Story

Established in 2000 in Telangana, Arrow Instruments started as a small, family-run venture with a simple goal: to provide reliable and precise instruments that professionals could truly trust. Over the years, we’ve grown into a name recognized for quality, innovation, and customer care, while never losing sight of our personal touch. From UV cabinets to temperature and pH indicators, every product we make is crafted with care, precision, and attention to detail, serving laboratories, industries, and educational institutions across the nation. At Arrow Instruments, we believe that technology should make life easier, not complicated, so we’re always ready to offer guidance, prompt service, and dependable solutions. Our true pride lies in the relationships we’ve built—with clients who come back not just for our products, but because they know we care about their success.

Quality Assurance

At Arrow Instruments, quality is at the heart of everything we do. Being a client-focused organization, we ensure that every product meets strict standards of accuracy, durability, and performance. Our dedicated team of quality controllers rigorously tests each instrument on well-defined parameters and carries out comprehensive calibration services.

  • Sending timely reminders for recalibration
  • Replicating calibrations using multiple methods
  • Correlating results for different characteristics of an item
  • Conducting regular internal quality checks using secondary reference materials
  • Participating in inter-laboratory comparisons and proficiency testing programs
